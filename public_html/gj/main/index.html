What is Game Jam?

Game Jam is a contest where participants utilize their skills and knowledges to make a game from scratch with a time constraint.

Participants might work independently, or in teams.

Participants are generally programmers, game designers, artists, writers, and others in game development-related fields.

Traditionally, game jam focuses on video games; however, board games have also been the subject of game jams. We are extremely exciting to see well-designed board games!!

    What is NetBit Game Jam?

    NBGJ is a game jam held by NetBit @UW, where we focuses to create and develop games that discuss on various topics such as justice, war and peace, and dystopia.

  • Maybe you want to start a project , but don't know how to produce a game as a Producer;
  • Maybe you just finish your CSE courses and can't find an exciting projects to keep using your passion on coding and interest in games;
  • Maybe you love to build 3D models, maps and characters for games and CGs, and want to utilize your works in real practice;
  • Maybe you are interested in game's art design and can produce exquisite art works for a game demo......

    • It's all okay. Every one is welcomed to participates NBGJ, no matter what position you choose, whether you are a beginner or "veteran".

    NetBit @UW will provide a brief introduction on how to start, and the steps that allows you to prepare as well as finish the jam with fun.

    Due to COVID-19, NBGJ will be held online, through Zoom (other platforms will also be supported after future announcement).
